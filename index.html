LinkedIn  /  Scholar

I'm a senior machine learning research engineer at Pipio AI where we're building foundation video model for talking humans. A video diffusion model with perfect lip sync, designed for video editing workflows..

Before that I was research associate at Fraunhofer HHI where I mainly worked on generation and animation of 3D talking head models using VAEs, 3DMMs, and neural rendering.

I did my PhD at HU Berlin, where I was advised by Peter Eisert.

Research

I'm interested in computer vision, deep learning, generative AI, and image processing.
